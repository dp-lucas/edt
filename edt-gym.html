When to Transition — 3 Signals
👃
Breathing test
Can you take one full breath through the nose and brace? Yes → go. Still gasping → wait.
💪
Bracing test
Before squatting or pulling: take a breath, create pressure, brace. Feels solid → go. Feels weak → wait 10 sec.
🎯
Quality check
"Can I do 5 clean reps right now?" Honest yes → go. Honest no → wait 10–15 sec and ask again.
